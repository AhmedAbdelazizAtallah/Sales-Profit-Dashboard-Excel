# 📊 Sales & Profit Dashboard (Excel)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Sales & Profit Dashboard built entirely in Microsoft Excel.

The dashboard analyzes retail sales performance across different years, categories, customers, states, and months to help business stakeholders monitor KPIs and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions

- What are the total sales and total profit?
- Which product categories generate the highest sales?
- Which states have the highest sales?
- How has profit changed over the years?
- Who are the top profitable customers?
- What are the monthly sales trends?
- How many customers purchased each year?

## 📈 Key Insights

- Technology generated the highest profit across most years.
- Phones and Chairs were among the top-selling sub-categories.
- Sales peaked during November and December.
- California recorded the highest sales.
- A small number of customers contributed a significant portion of total profit.
